<h3>Parents Support Group</h3>
<p>Kranji Secondary School Parents Support Group (PSG) was formally formed
on 1st September 2005. The PSG embraces the School’s vision, mission and
values and works collectively, and in partnership, with the School to support
the School’s endeavours, to enhance the stakeholders’ values, to enrich
the students’ learning experience and to foster close ties with the School
and Community.</p>
