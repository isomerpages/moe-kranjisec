<p>Dear 2026 Secondary 1 Parents/Guardians,</p>
<p>A very warm welcome to Kranji Secondary School! The Secondary 1 Registration
is to be completed online and electronically.</p>
<h3>Sec 1 Registration Site</h3>
<p>Please access the Sec 1 registration site here: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/sec1regkss" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/sec1regkss</a>
</p>
<p>You will find details on:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>1<sup>st</sup> day of school (2 Jan) and orientation camp (5-9 Jan)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Principal’s Talk and Campfire Finale (9 Jan)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Offering subjects at a more demanding level</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Class allocation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Purchase of school books &amp; uniforms</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Appeals</h3>
<p>If you would like to appeal into Kranji Secondary School, your PSLE score
must meet our school's cut-off point. Transfer will be subject to available
vacancies and the school’s admission criteria.&nbsp;Appeal form: <a href="https://go.gov.sg/2026sec1appeal" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/2026sec1appeal</a>
</p>
<p>The cut-off-point for 2026 Secondary 1 is:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Posting Group 3: 14</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Posting Group 2: 24&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Posting Group 1: 28&nbsp;</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Do note that if you do not hear from us by 2 Jan 2026, the appeal is deemed
unsuccessful. Students on the appeal list should still report to their
posted secondary school on 2 Jan 2026.</p>
